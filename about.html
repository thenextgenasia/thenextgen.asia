Who We Are ?

The Next Generation is a Company into Research & Development, finding solutions for a better tomorrow. We at the next generation firmly believe that every problem has a solution, and keeping the 17 Sustainable Development Goals of the UN in Mind, we ought ourselves to bring a change in society by finding the best possible solution to it. The company believes that the coming up generation of the nation is the future and they are the only one who could bring about a change in the society and this is what we all want to see, in order to give a platform to the youth, we at The Next Generation keeps doing a wide range of activities ranging from internship program to Workshops, Competitions to Events, so that every kid who is compassionate, has a passion towards doing something can come up and showcase his/her skills. The Next Generation is not just a community where you’ll get to learn something, but it’s a family of young minds, people who are compassionate to bring about a change in the current living world. We are building future leaders, so are you one of them?

Robotics

Being a hardcore technical community, robotics lies in heart of the company, exploring various trends like Machine Learning & Augmented Reality, we provide solutions to things that are pretty basic as well.
Events

Being involved in Robotics and Research, we as a community keep participating in Events and Compaetiotns those are recognised Nationally and Internationally.

Makerspace

It’s a space that will give you the opportunity to interact, ideate, innovate and build, as it’s much more than space, it’s a space of possibilities, where not only ideas are worked upon but they are actually implemented in the real world .


Research & Development

Being a start-up of the 21st Century, we firmly believe that Research & Development is an important step for anything that you do. After educating kids in workshops and seminars we highly encourage and support them to research upon their ideas and actually develop it in real life.

Education

As an evolving community, we are on a mission to promote STEAM Education PAN India. As responsible citizens of this nation, we should definitely do what is within our reach, in order to that we keep conducting workshops and boot camps for kids without any age barrier until and unless their passion speaks loud enough.

Branding

we give hand-in-hand support to the young geeky minds who have developed something that’s phenomenal but struggle to capture the public’s attention. From as basic to building websites and apps to connecting you with AV’s.Building your Network in order that you can create your net-worth.

So that's us. There's no other way to put it.
